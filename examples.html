Javascript Dice Game

A small Dice game written in Javascript.

This project allowed me to practice my Javascript skills, specifically to tighten my DOM manipulation and logical knowledge.

2 Players.

Roll the dice to accumulate score, hold your score to save it to your total and avoid losing your score to the hidden ruleset. First to accumulate 100 score points wins the round.

The highscore can be set via the 'Winning Score' text field.
